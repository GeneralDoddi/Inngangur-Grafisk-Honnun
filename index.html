JazzText

The Real Book

Authors unknown

Jazz text is an interesting font. Used in the Real Book, a book of sheet music of popular jazz music. Originally The Real Book was handwritten. It was imperative to the makers to keep the style of the original Real Book, thus this font was made

Though this text isn't exactly the best to read it has its own charm. It gives the feeling of authenticity, the feeling of expression, and eagerness to play some smooth music
